Key Highlights of the RBL Bank BankBazaar SaveMax Credit Card

The key highlights of the RBL Bank BankBazaar SaveMax Credit Card are as given below:

  • Convert all spends over Rs.2,500 into easy EMIs.
  • Xpress Cash facility for instant funds into your account.
  • Pre-approved loan without documentation or blocking of credit limit.
  • Add up to five complimentary cards.
  • Balance transfer facility with low interest rate and EMI options to repay other credit cards.
  • Up to 100% discounts using EMI Infinity Pass on the Split n Pay Fee.
  • Manage your credit card, pay utility bills, get merchant offers and instant loans through the RBL Bank MyCard App.
  • Contactless payments in a fast and secure manner of up to Rs.5,000 in retail outlets.

FAQs on RBL Bank BankBazaar SaveMax Credit Card

  1. How do I get my RBL Bank BankBazaar SaveMax Credit Card statement?

    2. You can view or download your RBL Bank BankBazaar SaveMax Credit Card statement through the RBL MyCard App, through the netbanking portal on the bank's official website, or on your registered mobile number given to the bank.

  2. Who can I add as a complimentary cardholder for the RBL Bank BankBazaar SaveMax Credit Card?

    3. You can add your spouse, parents, or children as complimentary cardholders of your RBL Bank BankBazaar SaveMax Credit Card (up to 5 add-on cards)

  3. How do I convert my purchases to EMIs on the RBL Bank BankBazaar SaveMax Credit Card?

    4. You can convert purchases above Rs.2,500 in value to EMIs through the RBL MyCard mobile application.

  4. How do I repay a loan taken against the RBL Bank BankBazaar SaveMax Credit Card?

    5. You can repay the loan taken against the credit card through Equated Monthly Instalments (EMIs) in different repayment tenures as per your convenience.

  5. How to register for the EMI Infinity Pass on the RBL Bank BankBazaar SaveMax Credit Card?

    6. You can register for the EMI Infinity Pass by giving a missed call from your registered mobile number to 8448290048.
